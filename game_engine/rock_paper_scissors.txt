ROCK_PAPER_SCISSORS MINIGAME INSTRUCTIONS
Based on the classic game, to play the
Rock_Paper_Scissors Minigame, simply:
1) Make a choice of 'rock', 'paper' or 'scissors'. The
CPU will also make a random choice.
2) Your choice will be compared to the CPU's to see who
won or if it was a draw.
3) The round's outcome and both yours and the CPU's
current scores will be displayed.
4) The game will end after a set number of rounds and your
score will be the number of rounds you win.
Enjoy the game!
